Karrimor Summit Mens Walking Shoes


The Mens Karrimor Summit Walking Shoes are perfect for your next outdoor adventure, designed with a moulded outsole with deep lugs and a wrap around toe box for added protection and traction on a range of surfaces. These karrimor walking shoes also feature breathable mesh panels to the upper, teamed with padded ankle collars and a lace up front for a secure and comfortable fit, finished off with the Karrimor logo to the side, heel and tongue.

> Mens walking shoes
> Laced
> Padded ankle collar
> Breathable mesh panels
> Moulded outsole with deep lugs
> Wrap around toe bumper
> Karrimor logo
> Leather / textile / synthetic upper, textile inner, synthetic sole
